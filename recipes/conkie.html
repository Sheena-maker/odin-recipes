Conkie

A Traditional Bajan Dish

Conkies are a longstanding tradition. Cornmeal and pumpkin based treats, prepared by steaming the mixture while wrapped in banana leaves.

Usually available through november, they can also be eaten year round

Ingredients

Collect the below ingredients from the supermarket. Don't worry about measurements

  • Cornmeal
  • grated coconut
  • brown sugar
  • Pumpkin
  • Banana leaves
  • Flour

How to Prepare

Now you have your ingredients lets make Conkies!

  1. mix everything except the banaleaves together
  2. add some butter
  3. wash then prep banana leaves by passing them across an open flame
  4. Cut prepped leaves into 8 inch squares
  5. put a dollop of the mixture into a banana leaf square
  6. Wrap and steam for 30 minutes
  7. Eat and enjoy!
