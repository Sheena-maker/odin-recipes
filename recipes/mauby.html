Mauby

A bittersweet bajan beverage

A drink made from the bark of the mauby tree. Traditionally brewed with a variety of spices.

In modern times Mauby if made from a prepared syrup.

Ingredients

The rum shop has the ingredients for this one too.

  • Sweet and Dandy mauby syrup
  • Water
  • Ice

How to prepare

You just need a cup and taste buds for this one.

  1. pour 1 part mauby into a large cup
  2. add 5 parts water to cup
  3. stir and add ice to taste
  4. Drink and enjoy!
