Cheese Cutters

A quick and easy bajan sandwich

Cheese cutters are super duper simple. They are often eated for breakfast or as a light snack

Best served with an ice cold Mauby

Ingredients

All of the below ingredients can be sourced from your nearest rum shop!

  • A 6 pack of Freshbajan saltbread
  • Popular supermarket no brand cheese
  • Barbadian Pepper sauce

How to Prepare

Absolutely no cooking skills needed

  1. Get softest bread
  2. Cut in half without fully separating bread
  3. Cut a generous slice of no brand cheese
  4. Place slice in nice warm bread
  5. Slather cheese with pepper sauce to your preference
  6. Eat and enjoy!
